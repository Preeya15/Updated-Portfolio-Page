About Me

Customer experience (CX) and Digital strategy are the underlying passions that drive my vision from product ideation, development, to delivery and implementation.

I started my IT career over 8 years ago. After finishing my Masters' degree in Information Technology, I joined NBN to be part of Build and Operate Australia's Wholesale broadband access network. My career started as Network Operator where I built and delivered processes for 4000+ DBYD (Dial Before You Dig) jobs, for which I was awarded with a "Megabyte Winner Award". This problem solving and inquisitiveness helped me in my career growth within NBN.

Working as a Product Owner/Technology Lead, I have a good understanding of Technology, Operations, IT SDLC, User experience and Customer experience. In my current job, I am accountable to develop a Product/Project from Ideation, impact analysis, defining business rules, stakeholder management, prioritizing backlog, sprint planning, solution designing, organizing workshops, act as interlock between Technical and Operations teams, implementation, and delivery.

When I am not on the job, I am out doing gardening. I believe in sustainable living and leaving less carbon footprints. I also have interest in Leadership/Management, so I am constantly either reading or listening to podcasts about it.

I am a firm believer in setting short-term and long term goals. Being able to consistently achieve and work towards the goal is highly motivating and drives me to deliver and constantly move forward.
